Privacy Policy

We believe privacy is a right. Here's exactly what data DailyPrep collects and why.

Last updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy describes what data the DailyPrep app collects and how we use it. We do not sell your data.

Data we collect

We collect the following data to improve the app and fix issues:

  • Crash and error reports — When the app crashes or encounters errors, we may send technical information (such as stack traces and device type) to our error-reporting service so we can fix bugs.
  • Usage analytics — We collect anonymous information about how you use the app, for example:
    • Which screens you open (e.g. practice, quick review, statistics)
    • Completion of key actions (e.g. finishing a practice session or onboarding)
    • General usage patterns to help us prioritize improvements

This data may include technical identifiers (e.g. device or session identifiers) that could, in combination with other information, be linked to you or your device. We do not require you to create an account or log in.

How we use the data

Our internal teams (engineering, product, marketing) use this data to:

  • Find and fix crashes and errors
  • Understand how users use the app
  • Make product and design decisions to improve the app

We do not sell your data to third parties. We use a third-party service (Sentry) to process crash and analytics data under strict data processing terms.

Your choice

When you first start loading content in the app, you will see a notice and can:

  • Accept — Allow us to collect crash reports and usage analytics as described above.
  • Opt out — We will not collect or send analytics or crash data. The app will work the same; we just won't receive this data.

Your choice is saved on your device. You can reset it (and all app data) via the app's Statistics / Reset option, which will show the notice again on next setup.

Data retention and security

  • Data is sent to our analytics provider and retained according to their policy (typically for error and product analysis).
  • We use industry-standard measures to protect data in transit (e.g. HTTPS).

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will be revised when we do. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at dailyprephelp@gmail.com.
